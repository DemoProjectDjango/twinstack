About Us

TwinStack Solutions is a dedicated Salesforce Non-Profit Consultancy, helping charities and mission-driven organizations leverage Salesforce for donor management, fundraising, volunteer coordination, and program tracking.

Our team of over 20 Salesforce specialists works with Nonprofit Cloud, Experience Cloud, Marketing Cloud, and custom integrations to simplify operations and improve efficiency. We assist non-profits in securing and fully utilizing Salesforce’s 10 free licenses through the Power of Us Program, ensuring cost-effective digital transformation.

At TwinStack Solutions, we design practical, scalable, and future-proof Salesforce solutions that enable non-profits to focus on their mission. From automating fundraising and donor engagement to streamlining grant management and reporting, we provide tailored solutions that drive measurable impact.

If you’re looking to optimize operations, enhance stakeholder engagement, or improve fundraising effectiveness, TwinStack Solutions is your trusted partner. Contact us today to explore how we can support your mission with Salesforce.
